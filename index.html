Sri Sithanandha Stores

Welcome to Sri Sithanandha Stores, a historic supermarket established in 1954 in the charming town of Lawspet, Pondicherry. With decades of experience, we have been serving our community with quality products and exceptional service. Explore our vast selection of groceries, fresh produce, household essentials, and more. Discover the nostalgic ambiance that takes you back in time while enjoying modern conveniences. Join us in continuing the tradition of providing a delightful shopping experience for all.

Why Sithanandha Stores ?

Quality

Sri Sithanandha Stores is committed to providing high-quality products to its customers. From fresh produce to household essentials, the store ensures that every item meets stringent quality standards, guaranteeing a satisfying shopping experience.

Tradition

With a rich history dating back to 1954, Sri Sithanandha Stores has become a beloved institution in Lawspet, Pondicherry. The store's longstanding presence and reputation for reliability and authenticity have earned the trust of generations of customers.

Convenience

Sri Sithanandha Stores prioritizes convenience for its shoppers. The store is well-organized, making it easy to navigate and find what you need quickly. Additionally, it offers a diverse range of products under one roof, saving customers time and effort by providing a one-stop shopping destination.
